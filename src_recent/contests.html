Photo & Art Contests



The SANCon costume photo competition is back.
This year we are excited to introducing a new category of digitally manipulated photos.

'World of Tomorrow Drawing Competition'
hosted by SANCon & Drawfest Christchurch

Put your drawing skills and creativity to the test! All you have to do is produce an original illustration, creatively interpreting the phrase "The World of Tomorrow". How you decide to do this is totally up to you. Entries will be judged primarily on their creative merit -so let your imagination run amok!

You may use whatever media you prefer; pencil and paper, paint and canvas, digital illustration, anthing goes --So long as your drawing is original, on a single page at least A4 in size (21 cm x 29.7 cm), and hand-drawn by you. Please consult the [rules and submission] guidelines for further details regarding participation.

Results and prizes will be presented on Sunday in the main panel room. The 'World of Tomorrow Drawing Competition' will be judged by representatives from 'Drawfest Christchurch', a drawing-focused social group based in Christchurch.

Competition Rules


  1. One entry per artist per category. Artists can enter more than one category.
  2. The competitions and categories are:
    • SANCon Customer Photo Competition
    1. Costume photographs
    2. Digitally manipulated costume photographs
    • Drawfest Christchurch ‘World of Tomorrow’ Art Competition
    • Produce an original illustration, creatively interpreting the phrase "The World of Tomorrow". How you decide to do this is totally up to you. -so let your imagination run amok!
  3. The judges and organiser reserve the right to switch categories or reject entries, if they do not fit the competition theme, are objectionable, offensive, or for any other reason. If in doubt ask an organiser.
  4. Digital retouching of the costume photos is allowed.
    • The Digital Manipulated photos should be a significantly different piece of art to the base photo. Two artists may contribute to the photo.
    • Art may be any media you prefer; Pencil and paper, paint, canvas, digital illustration.
  5. The photographer is responsible for either getting permission of the model, or ensuring they have appropriate copyright over the image.
  6. These competitions are open to all artists nationwide.
  7. If there are fewer than three entries in any category the judge or organiser may redistribute prizes.
  8. The Prizes:
    • To be announced.
  9. Each photo is judged on technical and artistic elements. Each artwork is judged primarily on their creative merit.
  10. The decisions of the judges are final.
  11. How to enter details:
    • Photo Competition - You must download the entry form from here and print it out and fill it in. Send the entry form and a photo between 12-24” on the longest side to 35 Everest Street, Burnside, Christchurch, by the 19th of September, or handed into the registration desk during Saturday of the convention.
    • Art Competition - You must download the entry form from here. The entry form and a single page artwork at least A4 in size (21 cm x 29.7 cm) must be handed into the registration desk during Saturday of the convention or be emailed to art@sancon.org.nz by the 20th of September.
    • For the entry forms, please right click the link and save, then open in Adobe Reader.
  12. Judging will take place on Sunday, and prizes will be handed out at 5pm in the main panel room. Prizes will be posted to those people not able to make it to the prize giving.
  13. You agree as a condition of entry that SANCon may use your artwork/photo for promotional purposes, including uploading to and display on the SANCon website, and in printed materials.
  14. The organisers' contacts are photo@sancon.org.nz and art@sancon.org.nz.
