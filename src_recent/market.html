Market Stalls


SANCon is an interactive popular culture convention, drawing on the resources of a number of University of Canterbury clubs. Our attendees are generally geeky teenagers or twenty-somethings, often University students or members of clubs, but last year we also received an appreciable number of families.

If this sounds like your target market, SANCon has a space open for you. The SANCon market will be running between 11:00am and 2:00pm on both Saturday and Sunday. It is free to run a stall. We can offer you a minimum of 2 metres of table space for your wares, as well as tables and chairs as required.
