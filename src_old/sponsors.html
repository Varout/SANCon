Sponsoring SANCon

SANCon 2013 is a multi-genre popular culture convention with a heavy emphasis on interaction and strong ties to the University of Canterbury student body. Last year was a financial and popular success despite a complete lack of advertising. This year we're planning on going bigger, with a comprehensive, multi-level advertising campaign and expanded events and facilities.

Our goal this year is for five hundred attendees - and these could be five hundred potential customers for your business. As a non-profit event, our goal is to provide the best experience to our attendees, and we'd love to have your help.

SANCon can offer advertising in a number of locations around the convention, including (as an example) posters in convention rooms, space on flyers and advertising brochures, sponsored events, and website space.

If you're interested in sponsoring SANCon, please contact our promotions officer for more information.
