SANCon Events

The heart of SANCon is its events. Last year, the organising committee ran a number of events for our attendees, including computer gaming, movie marathons, board gaming, combat demonstrations, roleplaying, medieval dance tutorials, comic panels, and more.

This year, we're throwing wide the gates for other clubs and individuals to run events at SANCon 2013. If you're interested in running an event over the course of SANCon, whether you represent a club or organisation or not, now's your chance to sign up.

Please note that the SANCon committee reserves the right to refuse your event, with or without an explanation. If you have any questions or further enquiries, please contact our Events Coordinator.
