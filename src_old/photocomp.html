Photo Contest

There will also be a Cosplay & Costume Contest at SANCon 2013. For more information on it click here.

Sponsored by Paul’s Camera Shop. Photos will be displayed in KE07. Details including entry are in the rules. Please read them. The prizes will be given out at the beginning of the costume competition, on the main stage.

Rules:

  1. One photo per photographer per category. Photographers can enter both categories.
  2. The categories are Costumes, and Alternative Fashion. As a guide Costumes include cosplays and other costumes, especially with heavy use of props, (including steampunk). Alternative fashions include Lolita, Cyber Goth, Vampire, Steampunk, other Goth and Punk genres, Visual Kei, or similar. Wearable arts, and non-alternative fashions are not permitted.
  3. Entrants are to exercise their judgement in the selection of which photo to enter in which category. However the judge and organiser reserve the right to switch categories or reject entries, if they do not fit the competition theme, are objectionable, offensive, or for any other reason. If in doubt ask the organiser.
  4. Digital editing of the images is allowed.
  5. The photographer is responsible for either getting permission of the model, or ensuring they have appropriate copyright over the image.
  6. This competition is open to all photographers nationwide.
  7. If fewer than three entries are received in either category the judge or organiser may combine both categories into one, and redistribute prizes.
  8. The prizes, courtesy of Paul’s Camera Shop in Christchurch, are vouchers worth: First place open $50, Second Place open $25, Best in Category $25 (for each category).
  9. The decisions of the judges are final.
  10. You must download and fill out all details on the entry form from http://sdrv.ms/12uwBA8. Please rename the entry from with your name. Then send an email to photocompetition@sancon.org.nz with the entry form attached by Friday the 20th of September. Photos need to be uploaded here. You may use any common format, e.g., TIFF, JPEG, etc. The photo may include a watermark. The files need to be named (Category)-(Title)-(AliasOrPreferedName).(file format) For example, Costume-Pikachu-GearPhotography.Tiff or AltFashion-SteamPilot-TimBurbank.JPG.
  11. You agree as a condition of entry that SANCon may use your image for promotional purposes, including uploaded to and display on the SANCon website, and in printed materials.
