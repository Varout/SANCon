Fashion Show

The sign up for SANCon's 2013 fashion show is finally open. Sign up here.

If your ideas change after you've signed up or you just want to touch up this or that on your character, feel free to let us know via e-mail and we'll get things updated for you. You can get hold of us at either: info@sancon.org.nz or fashion@sancon.org.nz.

Venue: The Undercroft at the University of Canterbury.
Time: Saturday from 7pm
Cost:

  • SANCon ticket holders: $5
  • Fashion Show only: $10
